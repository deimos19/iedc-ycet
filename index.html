IEDC YCET

OUR VISION

IEDC YCET envisions a thriving ecosystem of innovation and entrepreneurship, empowering students to transform ideas into impactful solutions. Through hackathons, ideathons, startup bootcamps, and mentorship, we bridge the gap between academia and industry, fostering creativity and technological excellence. Our mission is to nurture future innovators and entrepreneurs who drive real-world impact through cutting-edge solutions and sustainable ventures.

ABOUT US

IEDC YCET is a student-driven platform dedicated to fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and technological excellence. We provide opportunities through hackathons, ideathons, startup programs, and mentorship, enabling students to develop real-world solutions. By bridging academia and industry, we cultivate a culture of creativity, problem-solving, and startup growth, empowering the next generation of changemakers.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Our teams have actively participated in initiatives like Young Innovators Program (YIP) and Smart India Hackathon, showcasing their technical prowess and problem-solving skills. With a commitment to empowering young minds, IEDC YCET continues to be a hub for aspiring entrepreneurs and tech enthusiasts, driving impactful change in the world of innovation.
